About

CAMS Global is a developer of civil infrastructure, industrial, commercial and residential property in the developing world. Primarily active in Sub-Saharan Africa and Latin America, CAMS Global utilizes progressive building technologies to deliver the most cost effective, sustainable buildings and commercial real estate solutions available.

Primarily active in Sub-Saharan Africa and Latin America, CAMS Global utilizes progressive building technologies to deliver the most cost effective, sustainable buildings and commercial real estate solutions available.

CAMS Global works with leading international architects, master planners and technologists in conjunction with host-country professionals, leveraging global talent and resources to execute locally.

History

CAMS Global is a subsidiary of CAMS Group, a private equity and project development consortium that, among its other activities, has been a force in real estate development in emerging markets for some 37 years. Over its span, CAMS has developed employee and social housing, stadiums, hospitals, ports, hotels and other commercial projects throughout the Middle East, Latin America, Asia and Africa.
